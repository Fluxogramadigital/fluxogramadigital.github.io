ORB Turismo TT

ORB Turismo TT is a company focused on boosting local tourism. With the help of its partners, the points of greatest interest and which are desired to be visited are chosen. The connecting path of these points turns into a relaxed, pleasant and enriching route. The itinerary can be downloaded to the visitors' mobile device, using the ORB Turismo TT application.

The application for mobile devices developed has the objective of providing everyone with a guided visit to the most diverse locations. Its main objective is to boost local tourism, be it at a historical, gastronomical, cultural or landscape level, by designing routes that invite the user to know and enjoy all points of a defined route. It also intends to be able to boost local commerce through visits to various businesses.
